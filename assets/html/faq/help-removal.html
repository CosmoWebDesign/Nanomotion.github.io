Why is the Help Center going away?

The help center is being removed for many reasons.

Mobile Friendliness
The help site is not mobile friendly. It was rated 83/100 on testmysite.withgoogle.com.

Speed
The help site is very slow, especially on mobile devices. Mobile speed was rated 62/100 and desktop speed was rated 83/100. Page load times sometimes exceeded 5 seconds.

Inactivity
Google Analytics showed very little activity from users compared to other parts of the site.

Poor Structuring
The help site is very disorganized and confusing.

Will it come back?
No plans have been made to rebuild the help site in the near future.
