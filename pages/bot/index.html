Features

NanoBot comes with dozens of helpful features, such as moderation commands, a music player, and more!

MUSIC PLAYER

NanoBot comes with a music player that you can use to play all of your favorite memes, songs, and other videos. You can play music from over 100 websites, including YouTube, Vimeo, Mixer, and many more! You can view the full list of sites here.

MODERATION

Some features that NanoBot has are useful for moderation. You can kick and ban members with reasons and bulk-delete messages.

INFORMATION COMMANDS

Ever wondered how many people are in your Discord server, or don't remember when you made your account? You can find out with NanoBot's information commands. View user and guild info with just a simple command.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT

Encountered an error with the bot? No problem! Head on over to the Official NanoBot Discord server, and our friendly customer support will help you!
