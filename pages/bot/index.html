Introducing NanoBot 1.0

The 1.0 release of NanoBot will be the first full release since development first began in March and the public beta in May. By the time that this version is released, NanoBot will have many features, and all (if not, most) bugs will be patched. For now, while the public beta is still going on, you can try the beta version to help decide what will be added for the first release.

Features

NanoBot comes with dozens of helpful features, such as moderation commands, a music player, and more!

MUSIC PLAYER

I do not know about this "music", but I am programmed to assist where I can. NanoBot comes with a music player that you can use to play all of your favorite memes, songs, and other videos. You can play music from over 100 websites, including YouTube, Vimeo, Mixer, and many more! You can view the full list of sites here.

LIGHT MODERATION

Cease your resistance! Some features that NanoBot has are useful for moderation. You can kick and ban members with reasons and bulk-delete messages.

OVERWATCH STATS

I'm simply following my programming. NanoBot has a few commands involving the game Overwatch. View map and hero info, and even competitive stats!

FUN COMMANDS

Error 404: Sarcasm module not found. Need to look at some random dog or cat, or need some features to lighten up your server? NanoBot comes with commands for that.

INFORMATION COMMANDS

Current outlook? ‐ Optimal. Ever wondered how many people are in your Discord server, or don't remember when you made your account? You can find out with NanoBot's information commands. View user and guild info with just a simple command.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT

System malfunction. Encountered an error with the bot? No problem! Head on over to the Official NanoBot Discord server, and our friendly customer support will help you!
