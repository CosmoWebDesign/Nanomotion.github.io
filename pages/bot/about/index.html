About NanoBot

NanoBot was a project I started back in March of 2017. It was supposed to just be a small project I would do, put it in some of my friends' servers, and never release it to the public. It was in May when I had decided to release NanoBot to the public. I had added quite a few features to it, and I wanted other people to use NanoBot. There wasn't much in the v0.1-alpha release, just a help command and a music player. But, as time went on, more features were added. Most were suggested by the community at the Official NanoBot Discord. I owe most of my success to them. Now, NanoBot has over 35 thousand users. I never thought that many people would ever use NanoBot.

We'll see what the future holds.
- Der, the developer of NanoBot
7/8/2017
